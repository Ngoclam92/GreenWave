# 🌿 GreenWave (GWV)

GreenWave is a blockchain-powered initiative designed to empower communities to fund and support environmental projects worldwide. By combining transparent tokenomics with decentralized participation, GreenWave enables sustainable impact and green innovation.

---

## 🌍 Mission

GreenWave aims to bridge blockchain technology and environmental sustainability.

Our mission is to:

- Support climate-positive initiatives  
- Fund eco-friendly innovation  
- Promote transparent, decentralized funding  
- Build a community-driven sustainability movement
